Announcements
14.11.2023
ENTRY TO AITU TERRITORY IS ALLOWED TO AITU WORKERS AND STUDENTS ONLY WITH THE USE OF AN ID CARD.

Cases were identified of unauthorized persons entering the AITU building under the guise of students and creating conflict situations. In this regard, as well as in accordance with Chapter 13 of the AITU Internal Labor Regulations, entry to the AITU territory is carried out exclusively using an ID card of the established form.

Announcements
15.07.2020
Criminal liability for drug trafficking begins at the age of 16.

These are any actions as a result of which a person became the actual owner of drugs: drug production, stashes, purchase, appropriation of what was found, storage (even temporary) or collection of wild drug-containing plants.
